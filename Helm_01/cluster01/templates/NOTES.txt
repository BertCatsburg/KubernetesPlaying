- Configmap with a number of (fake) keys
- Simple Ubuntu pod for debugging called "shell"
- Simple Webserver deployment with 3 replicas showing a simple website.
- Gateway API to the Webserver

Access the webserver:
- From the outside, to the Gateway:   kubectl -n helmp port-forward svc/bertgateway-nginx  8080:80
- From the outside, to the Webserver: kubectl -n helmp port-forward svc/bertwebserver 8081:80
